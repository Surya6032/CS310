Abstraction Etymology:-
* The quality of dealing with ideas rather than events. 
* Freedom from representational quality in art.
* A state of preoccupation
* The process of considering something independently of its associations, attributes or concrete accompaniments.
* The process of removing something especially water from a river or some other source.
latin:- Abstrahere, Abstractio(DrawAway)
Abstraction- Representation with the removal of some level of implementation details.
